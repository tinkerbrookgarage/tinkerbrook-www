Setting up your Web and Email Fowarding
Logging In

Log in to your control panel, /cpanel
using the access details emailed to you when you placed your order.
Activating your eMail Fowarding

In the Email Section, click on Forwarders
Click on Add Forwarder
In the left box enter the name of the mail account you created previously, in the right
box enter the email address where you want your mail forwarded to.
Click on Add Forwarder
Click on [ Go Back ] until you are back to the main cPanel menu
Activating Your Web Forwarding

Click on Redirects in the Site Management Sub Menu
Enter the url where your website is hosted in the right hand side box.
Click on Add

Congratulations ! Your Web & Email Forwarding is now active, you may need to click your
browsers refresh button a few times to see your web site,
you can return to your cPanel at any time to modify your forwarding

Note: Some other features displayed on your cPanel menu may not be available, if you need
help scroll down to the bottom of the cPanel menu and click on Documentation
